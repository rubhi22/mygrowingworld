THE 2010 CLASSICS COLLECTION

Carroll Shelby is not just an automative success story...he is an american legend. His courage, ingenuity, tenacity and grit have made him the driving force for racing programs, products and industries bearing his name around the world.

Auto show and castrol presents a magnificient, comprehensive and spectacular 35,000 square foot exhibit of many of the exciting cars in which Carroll Shelby built his career, featuring up to 40 legendary Cobras and Shelby Mustangs that made his name Legendary.
